About the Brand

Every Loom & Grace garment starts life in a handloom workshop in Old Dhaka. The weaving process for a single dress takes 3–6 days. Their dye house uses only plant-based mordants. The brand has been carbon-neutral since 2022 and ships all Bangladesh orders in compostable packaging.

About the Brand

Thread Republic collects defective or surplus fabric rolls from RMG factories in Chittagong EPZ and respins it into high-quality knitwear yarn. Each top comes with a QR code linking to the exact batch of recycled input material used.

About the Brand

Nakshi Studio's fair-trade model guarantees artisans 60% of the retail price — triple the industry average. Each piece takes 8–20 days to complete and is unique. The studio trains 40 new artisans annually through a 6-month residency program.
